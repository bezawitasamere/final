Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guide hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayak is available for guest use

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails. We offer guided bird watching trips at sunrise several times a week.
